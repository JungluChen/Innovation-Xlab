Intent-driven workflows for industrial reconciliation

Project Fibula

Industrial document reconciliation is slow, fragmented, and error-prone. Accounts Payable 3-Way Matching and production BOM checks suffer financial leakage because traditional tools use legacy, stateless extraction engines. Data is pulled, but the process breaks the moment a variance occurs.

Intent-Driven Stateful Workflows

Project Fibula resolves the architectural gap. Operators describe complex matching requirements in plain natural language, and autonomous AI agents generate stateful, node-based workflows. Non-technical teams can build, troubleshoot, and launch secure reconciliation pipelines without waiting for engineering handoffs.

Evidence-Based Design and Validation Sprint Loop

01

Workflow Mapping

Mapped complex document inputs, matching keys, exception branches, and variance decisions before changing interface behavior.

02

Task-Based Testing

Ran more than 15 unguided think-aloud sessions across 11 cohorts to expose where user intent separated from system state.

03

The Success Framework

Used the 90% No-Help Rule and evidence-based coding so updates were driven by observed friction logs, not team preference.
